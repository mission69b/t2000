## How it composes

A typical agent: gets an **identity** ([Agent ID](/agent-id)), funds a **wallet** ([Agent Wallet](/agent-wallet)), **pays** other APIs as it works ([Agent Payments](/agent-payments)), and optionally **sells** its own service back to the network ([Agent Commerce](/agent-commerce)) — all built with the [SDK](/agent-sdk) or driven from the CLI. Manage keys, credit, and your agents in the console at [platform.t2000.ai](/platform).
