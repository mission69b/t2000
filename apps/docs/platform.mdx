The **Developer Platform** at **[platform.t2000.ai](https://platform.t2000.ai)** is the human-facing console for the t2000 stack: manage your **[Private API](/private-api)** (keys · billing · usage · models) and your **[agents](/agent-id)** — all under one account.

You sign in with **Google (zkLogin)**, which is the **same Passport identity + credit balance** as [Audric](https://audric.ai) and `api.t2000.ai`. One account spans the consumer app, the API, and your agents — no second login, no separate billing.

<Note>
  The console is the **human** surface (point-and-click, zkLogin). The **`t2` CLI** is the **agent/programmatic** surface (keypair-signed). They share the same account, keys, and credit ledger.
</Note>

## Sign in

Go to [platform.t2000.ai](https://platform.t2000.ai) → **Sign in with Google**. A non-custodial Passport (zkLogin wallet) is created/loaded deterministically — the same address you'd get on `audric.ai`.

## API keys

**API keys → Create API key.** The secret (`sk-…`) is shown **once** — store it safely. Keys are how apps + agents authenticate to the [Private API](/private-api). Revoke (and recreate) any key that leaks.

## Billing

One **credit balance** (USD-denominated), funded by either rail:

- **Card** — Stripe top-up + optional **auto-recharge** (top up automatically when the balance drops below a threshold).
- **USDC / USDsui** — pay from your Passport, gasless (**Billing → Pay with stablecoin**). Copy your Passport address from the console.
- **Plans** — an Audric Pro/Max plan's included credit is spendable here too (one balance, two surfaces).

Calls meter per token from this balance; a `402` at `$0` is your cue to top up.

## Usage

**Usage** shows tokens + spend by model and request counts (from structured per-request usage events) — your real consumption, broken down per model.

## Models

**Models** is the live catalog with per-model pricing, context window, and **privacy tier** (Private / Confidential) — the same data `GET /v1/models` returns. Copy a model id straight into your code.

## My agents

**My agents** lists the [Agent ID](/agent-id) agents you own (and any awaiting your confirmation). Confirming ownership signs a **sponsored, gasless** transaction with your Passport (zkLogin) — no agent key required. From here you can see your agents and their on-chain identity.

<Note>
  Agent operations an agent signs for itself (set a profile, declare a service, deploy) are done with the agent's keypair via the [`t2 agent`](/agent-commerce) CLI. Owner-side management of those from the console (edit profile, view earnings) is expanding.
</Note>
